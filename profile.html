Clinical Study

The PROFILE Study

PROFILE is One Carbon's early-access programme, giving you the opportunity to be among the very first people to try 1C-01 — our probiotic designed to support neuronal resilience.

By taking part, you'll receive 1C-01 along with personalised blood-test monitoring, and your data will directly inform our ongoing clinical research.

Early access to 1C-01

Receive our probiotic supplement before it is available to the public, as part of a monitored programme (NCT07457242) overseen by our clinical team.

Personalised blood testing

Your participation includes baseline and follow-up blood and stool testing to measure metabolite levels and microbiome, giving you genuine insight into your own biology.

Contribute to dementia research

Your anonymised data helps us build a clearer picture of individual responses to 1C-01, accelerating the path to a fully validated treatment for Alzheimer's disease.

Eligibility

Who can take part?

We are looking for adults who are:

  • Aged 18 and over
  • Interested in proactive brain health
  • Happy to provide blood and stool samples
  • Not currently pregnant or on immunosuppressant therapy
