Clinical Study

The PROFILE Study

Probiotic Research: Open-label Functional Intervention and Longitudinal Evaluation in Healthy Adults

PROFILE is One Carbon's phase one clinical trial for 1C-01, our probiotic designed to support neuronal resilience. In this trial, we are investigating probiotic survival in your gut and metabolic responses in your blood.

By taking part, you'll receive 1C-01 along with personalised blood-test monitoring, and your data will directly inform our ongoing clinical research.

Priority access to 1C-01

Receive our probiotic supplement as part of a monitored programme (NCT07457242) overseen by our clinical team.

Personalised blood testing

Your participation includes baseline and follow-up blood and stool testing to measure metabolite levels and microbiome, giving you genuine insight into your own biology.

Contribute to dementia research

Your anonymised data helps us build a clearer picture of individual responses to our one-carbon boosting probiotics, supporting our broader research into preventing Alzheimer's.

Eligibility

Who can take part?

We are looking for adults who are:

  • Aged 45 and over
  • Interested in proactive brain health
  • Happy to provide blood and stool samples
  • Not currently pregnant or on immunosuppressant therapy
