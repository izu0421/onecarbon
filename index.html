A Cambridge discovery

We found that boosting one-carbon metabolism protects the ageing brain.

Our journey started at Cambridge with a fundamental biological breakthrough. We discovered that a specific cellular network — one-carbon metabolism — is the master key to protecting mitochondrial function and sustaining long-term brain health. This discovery was so central to redefining cognitive care that it became our name: OneCarbon.

To harness this power, we engineered a living biological solution. We isolated a naturally occurring gut strain and refined it in our laboratory to continuously produce these essential one-carbon metabolites from within your own body.
