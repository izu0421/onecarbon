What we do

The one-carbon metabolic cycle is an important metabolic network in human biology regulating cellular function. Disruptions in this cycle are known to be associated with ageing and neurodegenerative disease. At One Carbon Technologies, we develop probiotics to support this cycle through natural evolution processes.
