By continuing to use our website you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw or change your preferences at any time — see Managing your cookies below.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit. They allow the website to remember information about your visit — such as items in your cart or your preferred language — and can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixel tags, and local storage. For simplicity we refer to all of these collectively as "cookies" in this policy.

Cookies are set either by us (first-party cookies) or by third parties whose services we use on our pages (third-party cookies).

How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep our website secure and functioning correctly
  • Process payments safely and prevent fraud
  • Remember your cookie preference
  • Understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it

Strictly necessary cookies do not require your consent under PECR and UK GDPR because they are essential for the website to function. All other cookie categories require your consent before we set them.

Managing your cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences at any time using the following methods:

Browser settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can typically find these settings in the "Options", "Preferences", or "Settings" menu of your browser. Instructions for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website, including the checkout process.

Opt-out tools

You can opt out of Google / YouTube's use of cookies by visiting Google's data and privacy settings, or by using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Strictly necessary cookies cannot be disabled without affecting the functionality of the website. We set these cookies on the basis of our legitimate interests in providing a secure, functional service.
