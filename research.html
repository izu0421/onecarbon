Our Research

Dr Yizhou Yu, One Carbon Labs' Founder, began his research journey undertanding how brain mitochondria are destroyed by Alzheimer's Disease. We found that one-carbon metabolic cycle disruptions were largely responsible for neuronal death.

The question then became: how can we protect these mitochondria?

Interestingly, most people absorb these metabolites as a part of normal digestion, and studies have shown people with stronger 1C metabolism express stronger neuronal resilience than average.

So, we evolved 1C-01, a probiotic bacteria, to produce these one-carbon metabolites in your gut. In an early pilot trial, our bacteria survived in every participants' gut. 1C-01 is also producing these 1C metabolites, but in varying amounts. We want to know why.

One Carbon integrates information at different levels of biology — genomic, metabolomic, proteomic, cellular, and population levels. Our novel mechanistic insights into dementia lead us to identify, refine and validate a naturally-occurring probiotic bacteria that might be able to delay or prevent Alzheimer's disease pathology.

Through natural evolution, we developed a probiotic strain that is able to substantially improve neuronal health in pre-clinical models of Alzheimer's disease.
