Introducing PROFILE: Our Phase I Trial

February 2026 · Clinical Research

We're excited to announce the launch of PROFILE — our first clinical trial, investigating the safety and biomarker effects of 1C-01 in healthy adults. PROFILE stands for Probiotic Research: Open-label Functional Intervention and Longitudinal Evaluation in Healthy Adults.

What PROFILE is investigating

PROFILE is a Phase I study. That means our primary goals are safety — confirming that 1C-01 is well tolerated — and early biomarker signals. Participants will provide baseline and follow-up blood and stool samples, giving us a picture of how 1C-01 affects one-carbon metabolite levels and gut microbiome composition over time.

This is not a therapeutic trial. We are not claiming that 1C-01 treats or prevents Alzheimer's disease or any other condition. PROFILE is the first step in a longer research journey.

Who can take part

We are looking for healthy adults aged 45 and over who are interested in proactive brain health. Participants need to be willing to provide blood and stool samples, and should not currently be pregnant or on immunosuppressant therapy.

What participants receive

Everyone who joins PROFILE receives 1C-01, personalised biomarker monitoring, and a report summarising their results. Your data — anonymised — contributes directly to our published research.

If you're interested, you can learn more and register your interest on the PROFILE page.
