How 1C-01 works

1C-01 is the product of years of One Carbon's research. The one-carbon cycle is your body's natural defense mechanism against diseases, including Alzheimer's. People with elevated one-carbon metabolism experience slower AD-related neurodegeneration. With 1C-01, we aim to boost this metabolism, helping your body fight Alzheimer's.

Our tests in animal and disease models demonstrated increased neuroresilience against Alzheimer's Disease after ingesting 1C-01. We are investigating now if the same effect occurs in humans. By purchasing a biomarker testing package, you will be helping us learn if 1C-01 can slow Alzheimer's Disease.
