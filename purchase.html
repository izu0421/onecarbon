What it is

A living probiotic, made to produce one-carbon metabolites

1C-01 is a food-grade probiotic. We isolated a naturally occurring gut strain and refined it in our Cambridge laboratory so that it produces one-carbon metabolites steadily, from within your own gut. One capsule a day, taken with water.

Questions

Before you start

Is 1C-01 a medicine? +
No. 1C-01 is a food supplement, not a drug. It has not been MHRA-approved to diagnose, treat or prevent any disease.
Does it treat or prevent Alzheimer's disease? +
No. 1C-01 is a food supplement and makes no disease claims. Our team's research explores the underlying biology of one-carbon metabolism and brain ageing, and that work is described on our research page.
Can I take it alongside my medication? +
1C-01 is a food-grade probiotic rather than a drug. If you are pregnant, breastfeeding or taking medication, please consult your doctor before starting.
Who should not take 1C-01? +
1C-01 is not suitable for children. If you are pregnant, breastfeeding or on medication, consult your doctor first. Do not exceed the recommended daily dose of one capsule.
When might I notice anything? +
Everyone is different, and as with any probiotic it takes time. The label notes that full benefits develop over 8 to 12 weeks of consistent daily use.
