Our story

A decade of work, traced along a single signal.

We started with a question no one could answer cleanly — and followed it, year by year, through labs, failures, and one very stubborn idea. Scroll to travel the length of it.

Scroll to begin
2014 — The seed

2014

A paper, a whiteboard, and a question that wouldn't leave.

Two founders meet at a climate workshop. They sketch the first version of what would become our core model on the back of a conference badge. It fits on one page.

2016 — First signal

2016

We run the pilot. Nothing works. Then something does.

Eighteen months of dead ends end in a three-line result that changes everything. We file the first patent and hire our fourth engineer.

2019 — Branching

2019

From one experiment to a platform.

The methodology generalises. We partner with three research institutions and publish the framework openly. Adoption is faster than we planned for.

2022 — Scale

2022

One hundred projects. Twenty-two countries.

We cross a threshold that felt theoretical five years earlier. The team is now forty people working across four continents, still shipping against the same one-page idea.

2024 — Depth

2024

The signal gets sharper, the stakes get bigger.

Our measurement layer goes open-source. A thousand researchers contribute inside twelve months. The network effect we modelled in 2016 finally lines up with reality.

2026 — Today

Today

The network keeps growing — and so does what it can do.

Everything we've learned is compounding. The next decade is about reach: getting this into the hands of the people and places that need it first.
